WHO IS THIS GUY?

I am Mithilesh Kumar, currently an active member of photography club at IIT ROORKEE, where I am currently pursuing my B.Tech. As a versatile man,capturing the unique vibrancy of everyday life through street photography, highlighting individual personalities in portrait photography, showcasing the intricate designs of buildings in architecture photography, depicting the serene beauty of the natural world in nature photography. I am honored to have received recognition from the Google Pixel team, and my work has been showcased at IIT Dhanbad during a photo exhibition.
